Cookies Policy

We use a handful of cookies to make your visit as warm and satisfying as our hearth-baked loaves. Here’s everything we sprinkle.

🍪

Our cookie philosophy

At Embermint Breadsmith House, we believe in minimal, honest tracking. We use cookies to keep the site functional, understand which pages our bakers love most, and to remember your preferences (like if you prefer rye over spelt). We never use invasive tracking or sell your data.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us improve your experience by remembering your actions and preferences over time. Think of them as breadcrumbs that make your journey smoother.

Cookies we use

Cookie name Type Purpose Duration
ember_session Essential Maintains your session and preferences (e.g., dark mode, language). Session
breadcrumb_pref Functional Remembers your favorite grain or baking style (optional). 1 year
_ga (Google Analytics) Analytics Helps us understand which pages are most popular (anonymized). 2 years
_gid (Google Analytics) Analytics Distinguishes unique users (anonymized). 24 hours
cookie_consent Essential Stores your cookie consent preferences. 1 year

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies from third-party networks. All analytics are aggregated and anonymized.

🍞 Your choice

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies via the settings menu. However, please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the site.

How to control cookies

  • Browser settings: You can adjust your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when they are being sent. Check your browser’s help section for instructions.
  • Opt-out tools: You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Our consent banner: When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie banner where you can accept or reject non-essential cookies.

Essential cookies

These are necessary for the site to function properly. They cannot be disabled in our system.

Performance & analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how visitors interact with our site. This helps us improve content.

Functional cookies

These remember your preferences and choices (e.g., your favorite bread type) to personalise your experience.

Third-party cookies

We only use third-party cookies for analytics (Google). They are GDPR compliant and data is anonymized.

Just like our bread, we keep things simple and honest. We don't over-bake our cookies — we use just enough to make your experience better.
