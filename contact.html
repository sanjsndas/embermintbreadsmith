Before you write

How fast do you reply?

Usually within 24 hours. If we’re deep in a bake, maybe 48 — but we’ll reply.

Can I visit the bakery?

Yes! We’re open Mon–Sat. Drop by for a loaf and a chat about sourdough.

Do you do workshops?

We host monthly hearth workshops. Send a message and we’ll share the calendar.

Bulk flour orders?

Yes — we source from small mills. Just ask, we’ll send you our wholesale list.
