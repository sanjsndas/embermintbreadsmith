About Embermint

🔥 hearth & heritage · founded 2018

Embermint Breadsmith House is a collective of bakers, writers, and fermentation enthusiasts. We believe bread is a living archive — of grain, climate, and human hands. Our work bridges traditional hearth baking with modern science, all in service of the perfect crust and an open, honeycomb crumb.

From our small bakery in the Pacific Northwest, we’ve built a global community of sourdough bakers who share our obsession with flour, water, salt, and time. This site is our journal, our lab, and our love letter to bread.

Our philosophy

We champion bread that is slow, honest, and deeply flavorful. That means long fermentations, locally milled grains, and a reverence for the natural microbiome. We don’t chase trends — we chase depth, texture, and the quiet joy of a well-baked loaf.

Our mission is to demystify sourdough and make artisan bread accessible. Whether you’re a first-time baker or a seasoned professional, Embermint is your companion in the journey from flour to fire.
