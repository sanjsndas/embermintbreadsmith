Privacy Policy

Your data is handled like a fine sourdough starter — with care, respect, and transparency. Here’s how we protect your information.

🔐

Our commitment

Embermint Breadsmith House (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We bake with integrity — and we handle your data the same way.

What we collect

  • Contact details – name, email address, phone number (when you reach out via forms or orders).
  • Baking preferences – if you share them, we may store your favorite grains or sourdough tips.
  • Website usage – anonymized analytics to help us improve the site (we use minimal cookies).

How we use your information

  • To reply to your messages, questions, and workshop bookings.
  • To send you our monthly bread letter (only if you opt in).
  • To improve our website and baking content.
  • We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

🍞 Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data anytime. Just drop us a line at hello@embermintbread.house – we’ll respond within 48 hours.

Cookies & tracking

We use a minimal set of cookies to keep the site functional and to understand which pages our bakers love most. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. For more details, see our Cookies Policy.

Data security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information. Our digital oven is locked.

Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed to serve you, or as required by law.

Third-party services

We use Mailchimp for newsletters and Google Analytics (anonymized). They are GDPR compliant.

Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. The latest version is always on this page.

Last updated: June 2026

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us – we’re happy to chat.

Trust in every crumb

We believe in transparency as much as we believe in a perfect oven spring. Your privacy is part of our recipe.
