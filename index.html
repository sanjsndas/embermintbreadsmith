    Frequently asked questions

    Why is my sourdough not rising?

    Check starter activity, water temperature, and bulk fermentation time. Use the float test.

    Can I use whole wheat flour in any recipe?

    Yes, but adjust hydration by 5-10% and allow longer autolyse. Whole grains absorb more water.

    What is the best scoring depth?

    ¼ to ½ inch, at a 30° angle. This creates an ear and controls oven spring.
